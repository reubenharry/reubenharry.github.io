Research

Reuben Cohn-Gordon – 26 June, 2018

My current work centers around Bayesian models of pragmatics. I’m interested in extending them to figurative language (particularly irony and metaphor) and sociolinguistic phenomena (indexicality), as well as scaling them in computationally tractable ways to handle the complexity of natural language.
